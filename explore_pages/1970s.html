Platform Boots

With widened pants come widened shoes. Platform boots were typical companions to the bell-bottom look. Like most things in the 70's they certainly made a statement.

Bell-Bottom Jeans

Bell bottom jeans first became popular in the 1960’s amidst a spirit of rebellion from the established rules and ways of living. Even now, bell bottom jeans are often one of the fashion items most associated with the 1970’s, a time rich in fashion trends and expressions.

The Originators of Bell-Bottoms

Initially a practical feature for sailors that allowed easy cuffing and rolling, or even inflatable floatation devices, bell bottoms became icons of the counter culture movement, eventually moving into mainstream fashion in the 1970’s.
